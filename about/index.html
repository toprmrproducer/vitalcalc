About VitalCalc

VitalCalc is a focused tool: calculating your Body Mass Index (BMI) from your height and weight, and showing where that number falls on the World Health Organization's standard weight categories — along with your ideal healthy weight range and BMI Prime. Everything runs entirely in your web browser, so no data you enter is ever sent to a server.

Because there is no server-side processing or account system, VitalCalc can stay completely free with no sign-up, no watermark, and no usage limits. The site is supported by on-page advertising.

BMI is a quick, population-level screening measure, not a medical diagnosis. It doesn't account for muscle mass, bone density, age, sex, or fat distribution. Always talk to a doctor or healthcare provider for a full assessment of your health.
