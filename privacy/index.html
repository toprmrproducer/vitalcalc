Privacy Policy

VitalCalc does not upload, store, or transmit any height, weight, or result you enter. All BMI calculations happen locally in your browser using plain JavaScript. Your data never leaves your device.

We do not require an account, and we do not save your inputs between visits unless your browser itself remembers form values.

This site may show advertising via Google AdSense, which can use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can control ad personalization in yourGoogle Ads Settings.

We do not use analytics that identify individual users, and we do not sell or share any data because we do not collect any of your health information in the first place.

Every calculation runs locally in your browser. Nothing you enter is ever sent to a server.
